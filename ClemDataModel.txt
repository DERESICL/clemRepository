The following data model will be designed to hold information relating to the holiday Home bookings and is defined as follows:-

Entities
    Clients
    Homes
    Bookings
    Home Facilities
    Price Bands
    Locations

The Entities are related as follows:

    A Customer can Book One/more homes
    A Home may have One/more Bookings made for it
    A Location may contain zero, One/more Homes
    A Cottage may have zero, One/more Facilities
    A Facility may be associated with zero, One/more Cottages
    A Home can have various prices associated with it 
    A range/rate can have zero, One/more homes
